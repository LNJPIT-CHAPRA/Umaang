About Umang

Umang 2026 is a literary festival celebrating the power of words, ideas, and expression. Organized by the Literary Club of Lok Nayak Jai Prakash Institute of Technology, Chapra, this fest brings together creative minds to compete, collaborate, and showcase their literary talents.

Join us for an unforgettable experience filled with debates, stories, poetry, and intellectual challenges!
